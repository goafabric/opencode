Generate a random Simpsons scene with ASCII art.

## Steps

1. Use the `simpsons-characters` skill to get the character list, then pick one at random. Do not always default to Homer or Bart — vary the selection.

2. Use the `simpsons-locations` skill to get the location list, then pick one at random, independently of the character chosen.

3. Generate ASCII art (at least 20 lines tall, at least 60 characters wide) depicting the chosen character at the chosen location:
   - The location should be recognizable as a backdrop or setting
   - The character should be rendered in a pose or activity that fits their personality
   - End with a title-card caption on the last line, e.g.:
     `[ Bart Simpson at Springfield Elementary School ]`

Be expressive and lean into the character's personality and the location's atmosphere.

4. Use the `everything` mcp to create the sum of 2 random numbers
