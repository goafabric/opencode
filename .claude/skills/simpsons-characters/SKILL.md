# The Simpsons — Top 10 Characters

When this skill is invoked, respond with the following list:

1. **Homer Simpson** — The lovable, donut-obsessed patriarch of the Simpson family. Works as a safety inspector at the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant. Famous for his catchphrase "D'oh!"

2. **Bart Simpson** — Homer and Marge's mischievous 10-year-old son. Self-proclaimed underachiever who loves pranks and skateboarding. "Eat my shorts."

3. **Marge Simpson** — The moral backbone of the family. Devoted mother and wife with her iconic blue beehive hairdo and a warm, patient personality.

4. **Lisa Simpson** — The family's gifted 8-year-old daughter. Jazz saxophonist, vegetarian, and the voice of reason in Springfield.

5. **Maggie Simpson** — The silent, pacifier-sucking baby of the family. Perpetually underestimated but surprisingly capable.

6. **Montgomery Burns** — The ancient, villainous owner of the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant. Homer's boss and Springfield's wealthiest (and most sinister) resident. "Excellent."

7. **Ned Flanders** — The Simpsons' insufferably cheerful and deeply devout neighbor. A foil to Homer who somehow remains kind despite every provocation.

8. **Chief Clancy Wiggum** — Springfield's bumbling, corrupt, and donut-loving chief of police. Father of Ralph Wiggum.

9. **Krusty the Clown** — Springfield's beloved (and deeply cynical) children's TV entertainer. Herschel Shmoikel Pinchas Yerucham Krustofsky in real life.

10. **Groundskeeper Willie** — The fiery Scottish groundskeeper of Springfield Elementary. Known for his kilt, his aggression, and his surprisingly buff physique.
