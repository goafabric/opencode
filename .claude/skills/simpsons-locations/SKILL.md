# The Simpsons — Top 10 Locations

When this skill is invoked, respond with the following list:

1. **742 Evergreen Terrace** — The Simpson family home. A modest two-story house in Springfield that has been the heart of the show since episode one.

2. **Springfield Nuclear Power Plant** — Homer's workplace, owned by Mr. Burns. A dangerously mismanaged facility that somehow keeps Springfield running.

3. **Moe's Tavern** — Homer's favorite bar, run by the gruff bartender Moe Szyslak. The go-to gathering spot for Homer and his friends.

4. **Springfield Elementary School** — Where Bart, Lisa, and their classmates suffer through Principal Skinner's administration and Mrs. Krabappel's classroom.

5. **Kwik-E-Mart** — Apu Nahasapeemapetilon's convenience store. Open 24 hours, frequently robbed, and the source of countless Squishees.

6. **The Android's Dungeon & Baseball Card Shop** — Comic Book Guy's domain. A treasure trove of collectibles and condescension.

7. **Springfield Retirement Castle** — Home to Grampa Simpson and Springfield's other elderly residents. Perpetually underfunded and understaffed.

8. **First Church of Springfield** — Reverend Lovejoy's congregation, where the Flanders family devoutly worships and the Simpsons occasionally show up.

9. **Lard Lad Donuts** — Springfield's iconic donut shop, recognizable by its giant fiberglass mascot holding an oversized donut. A Homer favorite.

10. **Springfield Penitentiary** — The local prison, frequently visited by Sideshow Bob and other recurring Springfield criminals.
